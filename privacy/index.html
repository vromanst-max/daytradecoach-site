DayTrade Coach - Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 June 2026

DayTrade Coach ("the game") is developed by RagnarBeaucoup ("we", "us"). This policy explains what personal data the game collects, why, and how it is handled. If you have questions, contact us at ragnarbeacoup@gmail.com.

Data we collect

When you sign in through Epic Account Services / Epic Online Services (EOS), we receive a limited set of account information from Epic Games:

  • Your Epic Account ID and account display name;
  • An EOS Product User ID used to record your in-game achievements and progress.

Your game progress and settings (save files) are stored locally on your own device. We do not collect your name, address, payment details, or other sensitive personal information, purchases are handled entirely by the store (Epic Games Store / Steam).

How we use it

This information is used only to sign you in, display your name in-game, and unlock and track achievements. We do not use it for advertising and we do not sell your data.

Third parties

The game uses Epic Online Services, provided by Epic Games, Inc., to deliver sign-in and achievements. Epic's handling of your data is governed by Epic's own privacy policy: epicgames.com/site/privacypolicy. We do not share your data with any other third parties.

Data retention and your rights

Account-linked achievement data is retained while you use the game. You may request access to, or deletion of, the data associated with your account by emailing ragnarbeacoup@gmail.com. Save files can be removed at any time by deleting them from your device.

Children

DayTrade Coach is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date above reflects the most recent change.
