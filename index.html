About the game

Day Trade Coach is a day trading simulator built for focus. Cut through the noise, master the set-ups that actually work, and build your edge, one trade at a time. Trade a living market of fictional tickers in a modern terminal — read the chart, time your entry, manage your risk, and learn from every result.

Remember, you don't need to master every strategy to be a successful trader. If just one works for you consistently, you can become profitable. You don't need to win every trade — you just need to develop an edge.

Features

  • Modern terminal UI — a clean, professional trading workspace.
  • Intraday market engine tuned specifically for day-trading set-ups.
  • Pro-grade indicators — MACD, RSI, VWAP, EMAs and more.
  • Guided strategies & alerts to build real pattern recognition.
  • Real time or fast-forward through the trading session.
  • Full order types — market, limit, and stop-loss orders.
  • Objectives that unlock margin and short selling as you progress.
  • Live news feed where events move the market.
  • Trading Journal & Replay — replay any trade from your entry and try a different call to see how it would've played out.
  • Coach Mode — an optional helper that walks you through each strategy.
